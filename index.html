Welcome to My Website

I am a marine biologist focusing on the detection of native UK seahorse species using passive acoustic monitoring and environmental DNA (eDNA). Explore my work and research on this site.

About Me

Hi, my name is Russell Connelly, a Marine Biologist specializing in the detection of native UK seahorse species using passive acoustic monitoring and environmental DNA (eDNA). I have a passion for marine life and conservation, and I am dedicated to advancing our understanding of these fascinating creatures.

Research & Education

My research focuses on the use of innovative techniques to monitor and protect seahorse populations in the UK. I also engage in educational outreach to raise awareness about marine conservation.
