About Me

I am a marine biologist with a PhD focusing on detecting native UK seahorse species using passive acoustic monitoring combined with environmental DNA (eDNA). My research aims to enhance conservation efforts by developing non-invasive techniques for monitoring seahorse populations, providing vital data for species protection and habitat preservation.
