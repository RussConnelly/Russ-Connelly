Seahorses found!

Oct 08, 2024

Summer fieldwork was a success! This summer, we headed down to Studland Bay, Dorset with the intention to trial my techniques for the project. In total, we found 8 H. guttulatus, with a couple of the males being very much pregnant! I managed to secure hundreds of hours of passive audio from the bay and sediment from nearby seahorses.

Stay tuned for more updates!
