About Me

Hi, my name is Russ Connelly, a Marine Biologist specializing in the detection of native UK seahorse species using passive acoustic monitoring and environmental DNA (eDNA). I have a passion for marine life and conservation, and I am dedicated to advancing our understanding of these fascinating creatures.

Research & Education

My research focuses on the use of innovative techniques to monitor and protect seahorse populations in the UK. I also engage in educational outreach to raise awareness about marine conservation.
