About Me

I am a marine biologist focusing on the detection of native UK seahorse species using passive acoustic monitoring and environmental DNA. My research aims to develop non-invasive monitoring techniques for species protection and conservation.

Research

My current work involves using passive acoustic monitoring (PAM) to detect the presence of UK seahorse species. By combining PAM with environmental DNA analysis, I aim to gather data that can support the conservation of these endangered species.
